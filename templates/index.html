About Alcohol & Phenol Reactions

Common Alcohol Reactions

  • Oxidation: Primary alcohols → aldehydes → carboxylic acids
  • Oxidation: Secondary alcohols → ketones
  • Dehydration: Alcohols → alkenes + water
  • Halogenation: Alcohols + HX → alkyl halides + water
  • Esterification: Alcohols + carboxylic acids → esters + water

Phenol Reactivity

  • Electrophilic Substitution: Phenols undergo substitution at ortho/para positions
  • Acidity: Phenols are more acidic than alcohols
  • Oxidation: Complex reactions depending on oxidizing agent
  • Esterification: Phenols + acid chlorides → phenyl esters
  • Kolbe Reaction: Phenols + CO₂ → salicylic acids
